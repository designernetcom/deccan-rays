Who We Are

DECCAN RAYS SOLAR &
ENERGY PROJECTS LLP

We are one of the leading energy solution providers. With the goal of bringing renewable and non-polluting power to homes and business units in India and around the world. Deccan Rays Solar is involved in activities like sales, installation, and servicing of Solar Power System for reliable power supply. The company educates customer about the importance and helpful use of Solar Power System for customer to save money as well as The Mother Earth from Global Warming.


We provide total solution for Electric Power Supply from Solar for customer. We help our customers to realize all different benefits of renewable energy, such as Solid financial returns and a positive impact on the environment, through a unique Infinite energy to all approach.
